Google LLC is an American multinational technology company that specializes in internet-related services and products. Google's products include: Online advertising Search engine technology Cloud computing Computer software Quantum computing E-commerce Consumer electronics Artificial intelligence (AI) Google's products and services are used by billions of customers across the world. Some of Google's products include: Google Search, Google Chrome, Google Docs, Google Calendar, Google Photos, Google Meet, Google Drive, Google Finance, and Google Play Books. Google's mission statement is "committed to significantly improving the lives of as many people as possible". The company's core values include protecting users, building belonging, expanding opportunity, responding to crises, and advancing sustainability. Google was founded in 1998 by Sergey Brin and Larry Page. Since 2015, Google has been a subsidiary of the holding company Alphabet, Inc.. Google has a business presence across the Americas, Europe, and Asia.
